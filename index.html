About Us

Z&S Infratech is a trusted name in civil and electrical infrastructure. Our dedicated professionals and years of experience contribute to India's critical infrastructure projects.

We specialize in complex urban infrastructure, including underground station construction and high-voltage electrical installations, partnering with the Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation.

Currently, we are working on major metro stations including Kalbadevi, Girgaon, and Grant Road, setting new benchmarks in engineering excellence.

Our Services

  • Civil Finishing – Flooring, Tiling, Painting, Ceiling
  • Electrical Works – Generator Installation, Power Distribution Panels (PDBs, DBs, MCCs)
  • Lighting Systems – Outdoor, Indoor, Emergency
  • Lightning Protection & Fire Alarm Systems
  • Substation Work & Temporary Electrical Supply
  • Installation of Motors, Cable Trays, Control Panels
  • Testing and Commissioning of Electrical Systems
